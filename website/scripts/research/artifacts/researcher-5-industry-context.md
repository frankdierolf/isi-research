# Researcher 5: Industry Context & Market Landscape

## Research Objective

Conduct a comprehensive analysis of the AI image editing market, competitive landscape, and industry trends. Your goal is to position ISI Voice Image within the broader context of AI-powered creative tools—identifying where voice-controlled image editing fits in the market, what competitors are doing, and where the industry is heading. This research will inform both the strategic framing of the feasibility study and the discussion of future opportunities.

---

## Project Context: ISI Voice Image

You are researching for a feasibility study on **ISI Voice Image**, a desktop application prototype positioned within a rapidly evolving market:

- **What it does**: Voice-controlled clipboard image transformation
- **Core workflow**: Copy image → Cmd+Shift+I → Speak instruction → Cmd+Shift+I → Paste transformed result
- **Development timeline**: Built in 48 hours (January 22-23, 2026)
- **Organization**: "Ulm Institute of Spoken Intelligence" (ISI)
- **Technology stack**: Tauri/Rust + Vue.js + Deepgram Nova-2 + Gemini 2.0 Flash
- **Measured latency**: ~2-3 seconds end-to-end

### Unique Positioning Elements
| Aspect | ISI Voice Image | Typical AI Image Tools |
|--------|-----------------|------------------------|
| Input method | Voice commands | Text prompts, buttons |
| Integration | System-wide clipboard | Browser/app-specific |
| Deployment | Desktop application | Web-based |
| Workflow | Hotkey-triggered | Tab/window switching |
| Latency | ~2-3 seconds | Varies (5-30+ seconds) |

Your research should explore: Who else is doing voice + image? What are the major AI image tools doing? Where is the market heading?
